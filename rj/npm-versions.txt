~2.3.0 -> installs greater than 2.3.0 but less than 2.4.0 
^2.3.0 -> installs greater than 2.3.0 (2.4.0, 2.3.1, 2.4.1, etc... first decimal is fixed, but the second and third
can be latest, it will NOT INSTALL version 3.0.0 if available)
* -> just star gives what ever the version is available greater than 0.0.0 will be installed
